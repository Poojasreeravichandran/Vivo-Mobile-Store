Troubleshooting

If you're facing issues with your Vivo phone, follow these common troubleshooting steps:

  • Check if the phone is fully charged or plugged in.
  • Restart your device to resolve minor software issues.
  • Ensure your software is up to date by checking for any updates.
  • If your screen is frozen, try holding the power button for 10-15 seconds.
  • If you're experiencing network issues, try toggling Airplane mode on and off.

Warranty Information

Vivo offers a one-year warranty on all smartphones purchased from authorized dealers. The warranty covers:

  • Manufacturing defects.
  • Hardware malfunctions (excluding accidental damage).
  • Battery defects (if not caused by improper use).

For more detailed warranty information, please contact our support team or visit your nearest Vivo service center.
